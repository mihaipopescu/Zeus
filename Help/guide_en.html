Who is Zeus ?

Zeus is the acronym for Zip Encryption Unified Shell extension.

In one phrase, Zeus is an application which integrates with Windows Explorer TM and which assures the protection through encryption and compression of the folders in an opened format namely the ZIP format at which it is added the new AES encryption extension formulated by WinZIPTM. The protected files are accesible also through the opening with WinZIP or with another utilitarian software which implements the encryption extension as 7-zip.
